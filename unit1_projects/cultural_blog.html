I screwed up.

The video was great. I had a ton of ideas flowing through my head. I love the re-evaluation of education that is happening at DBC and elsewhere. I am thrilled about the focus on accountability and internal motivation. So much so that I wrote a ton on the subject.

Then I deleted it.

On accident of course. I was working late, failed to commit changes to git, and then renamed a file and wrote over all my work. Sublime figured I knew what I was doing. Oops.

I wrote it for me.

My reflections are my own and I don't plan to rewrite them. I explored my feelings and motivations; the point of the exercise. One of the things I have learned is that I am here to learn, not earn a grade. I can spend another 45 minutes rewritting my feelings or I can move on to learn something else. When I put those two on a scale, the answer seems obvious.
